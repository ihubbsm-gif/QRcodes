<div class="card blue full-width">

Science Explained
</div>
<div class="card-body">

<p>In a right-angled triangle, the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides.</p>

<div class="formula-box">
<p>h² = a² + b²</p>
<ul>
<li>📐 h = Hypotenuse (side opposite the 90° angle)</li>
<li>📏 a = Altitude</li>
<li>📏 b = Base</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p>The model demonstrates that the combined area of the two smaller squares equals the area of the larger square. Squares with sides a and b together have the same area as the square with side h.</p>

<div class="proof-box">
<p>3² + 4² = 5² → 9 + 16 = 25 ✅</p>
</div>

</div>

<!-- WORKING & OBSERVATION -->
<div class="card orange">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-eye"></i>
Working and Observation
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>The exhibit uses three transparent square containers with sides of 3 cm, 4 cm, and 5 cm.</li>
<li>The smaller containers are filled with coloured water.</li>
<li>When the model is rotated, the squares open at one corner.</li>
<li>The coloured water flows into the larger square.</li>
<li>Observe that the larger square is completely filled by the combined water from the two smaller squares.</li>
<li>This visually demonstrates that 3² + 4² = 5² → 9 + 16 = 25.</li>
<li>The model provides a visual proof of the Pythagorean Theorem.</li>
</ul>
</div>
