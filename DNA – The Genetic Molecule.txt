<!-- SCIENCE INVOLVED -->
<div class="card blue">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-lightbulb"></i>
Science Involved
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>DNA is composed of nucleotides containing a sugar molecule, a phosphate group, and a nitrogenous base.</li>
<li>The DNA double helix consists of two sugar-phosphate backbones and complementary base pairs joined by hydrogen bonds.</li>
<li>Base pairing follows a specific pattern: A pairs with T, and C pairs with G.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<!-- WORKING -->
<div class="card purple">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-cog"></i>
Working
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>DNA stores genetic information in the sequence of its nitrogenous bases.</li>
<li>Complementary base pairing (A-T and C-G) holds the two strands together.</li>
<li>During replication, the strands separate and serve as templates for the formation of new DNA molecules.</li>
<li>The genetic information carried by DNA controls the structure and function of living organisms.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
