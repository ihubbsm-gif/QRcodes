About the Respiratory System
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>The human respiratory system is crucial for oxygenating the blood and removing carbon dioxide, a vital process for sustaining life.</li>
<li>It involves a complex network of structures that work together to ensure efficient gas exchange.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<!-- LEARNING OUTCOMES -->

<div class="card-body">

<li><strong>Educational Value:</strong> Learning about the respiratory system highlights the importance of efficient gas exchange and its role in overall health and homeostasis.</li>
</ul>

<!-- STRUCTURE -->
<div class="card purple full-width">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-shapes"></i>
Structure
</div>
<div class="card-body">

<div class="part-box">
<h4>👃 Nose and Nasal Cavity</h4>
<ul>
<li>Air enters through the nostrils, where it is filtered, warmed, and humidified.</li>
<li>The nasal cavity, lined with mucous membranes and cilia, traps dust and microbes.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🔗 Pharynx</h4>
<ul>
<li>The throat, divided into Nasopharynx, Oropharynx, and Laryngopharynx.</li>
<li>Serves as a passage for air from the nasal cavity to the larynx and oesophagus.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🎙️ Larynx</h4>
<ul>
<li>Known as the voice box.</li>
<li>Contains the vocal cords.</li>
<li>Prevents food from entering the airway while directing air to the trachea.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🫁 Trachea</h4>
<ul>
<li>The windpipe, supported by cartilage rings.</li>
<li>Carries air from the larynx to the bronchi.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🌿 Bronchi and Bronchioles</h4>
<ul>
<li>The trachea splits into bronchi, which branch into smaller bronchioles.</li>
<li>Distribute air to the lungs.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🫁 Lungs</h4>
<ul>
<li>The primary organs of respiration.</li>
<li>Consist of the Right Lung (three lobes) and Left Lung (two lobes).</li>
<li>Contain alveoli for gas exchange.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🔵 Alveoli</h4>
<ul>
<li>Tiny air sacs where oxygen enters the blood and carbon dioxide is removed.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>💪 Diaphragm and Intercostal Muscles</h4>
<ul>
<li>Contract to expand the chest cavity.</li>
<li>Draw air into the lungs.</li>
</ul>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<!-- WORKING -->
<div class="card orange">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-cog"></i>
Working
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Air enters the body through the nose and passes through the pharynx, larynx, and trachea.</li>
<li>The trachea carries air into the bronchi and bronchioles of the lungs.</li>
<li>In the alveoli, oxygen diffuses into the blood while carbon dioxide moves from the blood into the lungs.</li>
<li>The diaphragm and intercostal muscles help in inhalation and exhalation by expanding and contracting the chest cavity.</li>
<li>Carbon dioxide is expelled from the body during exhalation.</li>
</ul>
</div>
