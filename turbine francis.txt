Principle of Francis Turbine
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Converts hydraulic energy into mechanical energy.</li>
<li>Water enters through a spiral casing.</li>
<li>Guide vanes regulate the water flow.</li>
<li>Water strikes the curved runner blades.</li>
<li>The runner rotates and transfers mechanical energy.</li>
<li>The shaft drives a generator to produce electricity.</li>
</ul>
</div>
