<!-- SCIENCE INVOLVED -->
<div class="card blue">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-lightbulb"></i>
Science Involved
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Persistence of vision is a phenomenon in which the eyes and brain retain an image for a short time after it disappears.</li>
<li>An image remains on the retina for about 1/16th of a second.</li>
<li>This duration is known as the persistence of vision.</li>
<li>The human eye can process only a limited number of images per second.</li>
<li>Typically, the eye processes around 10 to 12 images per second.</li>
<li>When images are displayed faster than this rate, the brain blends them together.</li>
<li>This creates the illusion of continuous motion.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
