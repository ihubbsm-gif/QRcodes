AUTOMATION

(Manufacturing Industries)

Academic Information

Class: 10

Book Name: Contemporary India – II

Chapter: Manufacturing Industries

Learning Objectives
  • Understand the concept of automation and how machines and control systems perform tasks with little or no human effort.
  • Learn about its importance, working, advantages, and applications in industries and daily life.
Principle
  • Automation uses machines, sensors, computers, and control systems to perform tasks automatically.
  • It improves productivity, accuracy, consistency, and safety while reducing manual effort.
Working
  • Sensors collect information from the surroundings and send it to a control system.
  • The control system processes the data and gives commands to machines or robots.
  • These machines perform the required task automatically with minimum human intervention.
Applications
  • Automated manufacturing in factories for faster and accurate production.
  • Home and building automation for lighting, security, and appliances.
  • Automated traffic and highway management systems.
  • Video surveillance and security monitoring.
  • Industrial and laboratory automation for safe and precise operations.
