<div class="card orange">
<div class="card-header">

Working
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Heat from inside the Earth warms water underground.</li>
<li>Hot water and steam rise through wells.</li>
<li>The steam spins a turbine.</li>
<li>The turbine drives a generator.</li>
<li>The generator produces electricity.</li>
<li>The cooled water can be reused underground.</li>
</ul>
