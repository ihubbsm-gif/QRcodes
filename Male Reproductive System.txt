<!-- STRUCTURE -->
<div class="card purple full-width">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-shapes"></i>
Structure
</div>
<div class="card-body">

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🔵 Testes</h4>
<ul>
<li>The primary male reproductive organs located in the scrotum.</li>
<li>Responsible for sperm production and hormone secretion, particularly testosterone.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🌀 Epididymis</h4>
<ul>
<li>A coiled tube located on the back of each testis.</li>
<li>Where sperm mature and are stored.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>➡️ Vas Deferens</h4>
<ul>
<li>A muscular tube that transports sperm from the epididymis to the ejaculatory duct.</li>
<li>Also plays a role in sperm storage.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>💧 Seminal Vesicles</h4>
<ul>
<li>Glands that secrete a significant portion of the seminal fluid.</li>
<li>Provide nutrients to sperm and aid in their mobility.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>⚪ Prostate Gland</h4>
<ul>
<li>Located below the bladder.</li>
<li>Produces a fluid that nourishes and helps propel sperm during ejaculation.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>💧 Bulbourethral Glands (Cowper's Glands)</h4>
<ul>
<li>Small glands that secrete a lubricating fluid into the urethra.</li>
<li>Prepare the urethra for the passage of sperm.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🔗 Urethra</h4>
<ul>
<li>The duct that runs through the penis.</li>
<li>Carries both urine and semen out of the body.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>📍 Penis</h4>
<ul>
<li>The external organ that delivers sperm into the female reproductive tract.</li>
<li>Consists of three main parts: Root, Body (shaft), and Glans (head).</li>
</ul>
</div>
