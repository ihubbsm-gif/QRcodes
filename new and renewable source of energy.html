NEW & RENEWABLE SOURCES OF ENERGY

(Minerals and Energy Resources)

Academic Information

Class: 10

Book Name: Contemporary India – II

Subject: Geography

Chapter: Minerals and Energy Resources

Learning Objectives
  • Describe sources and uses of energy.
  • Define renewable and non-renewable energy.
  • Provide examples of common types of renewable and non-renewable resources.
  • Understand and explain general ways to save energy at personal, community, and global levels.
Principle Behind Exhibit
  • Provide solutions to the energy crisis by suggesting new and renewable sources of energy as replacements for existing sources like fossil fuels.
Concept and Applications
  • Transition to Sustainable Energy: Oil and gas scarcity necessitates the transition to renewable sources.
  • Solar Power: Harnessing sunlight for electricity.
  • Wind Power: Using wind turbines for energy.
  • Hydropower: Generating electricity from flowing water.
  • Geothermal: Using Earth's heat for power.
Importance
  • Environmental Benefits: Reduces carbon emissions and minimizes environmental impact.
  • Energy Security: Provides a stable and sustainable energy supply for the future.
