<div class="card-body">

<div class="sub-box">
<h4>☕ Coffee Cultivation</h4>
<ul>
<li>Coffee is a plantation crop grown mainly in tropical and subtropical regions.</li>
<li>The two most common species are Arabica and Robusta.</li>
<li>Coffee requires moderate temperature, adequate rainfall, and well-drained fertile soil.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="sub-box">
<h4>🌍 Geographical Importance</h4>
<ul>
<li>Major coffee-producing countries include Brazil, Vietnam, Colombia, and India.</li>
<li>In India, coffee is mainly grown in Karnataka, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="sub-box">
<h4>💰 Economic Importance</h4>
<ul>
<li>Coffee is an important commercial crop and export commodity.</li>
<li>It provides employment in farming, processing, transportation, and trade.</li>
<li>Coffee contributes significantly to national and international trade.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="sub-box">
<h4>🌱 Environmental Impact</h4>
<ul>
<li>Sustainable coffee farming helps conserve soil and biodiversity.</li>
<li>Unsustainable practices may lead to deforestation, soil erosion, and habitat loss.</li>
</ul>
</div>
