About Human Evolution
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Human evolution is the scientific study of the origins and development of the human species.</li>
<li>It encompasses the changes that occurred over millions of years leading to the modern human, Homo sapiens, from early primate ancestors.</li>
<li>This field integrates evidence from paleontology, genetics, and anthropology to understand the complex process of human development.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<!-- STRUCTURE AND KEY STAGES -->
<div class="card purple full-width">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-dna"></i>
Structure and Key Stages
</div>
<div class="card-body">

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🐒 Early Primate Ancestors</h4>
<ul>
<li>Human evolution begins with small, arboreal primates that appeared around 60 million years ago.</li>
<li>These primates were adapted for grasping and climbing.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🚶 Hominins</h4>
<ul>
<li>Around 7 million years ago, hominins emerged, leading to humans.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🦴 Australopithecus</h4>
<ul>
<li>Lived between 4 and 2 million years ago.</li>
<li>Exhibited bipedalism and a mix of human-like and ape-like traits.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🧠 Homo habilis</h4>
<ul>
<li>Appeared around 2.4 million years ago.</li>
<li>Had an increased brain size and used simple tools.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🔥 Homo erectus</h4>
<ul>
<li>Emerged around 1.9 million years ago.</li>
<li>Showed further brain expansion.</li>
<li>Used advanced tools and fire.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="part-box">
<h4>🧑 Neanderthals and Early Modern Humans</h4>
<ul>
<li>Neanderthals lived in Europe and Asia and were closely related to modern humans.</li>
<li>Anatomically modern humans appeared in Africa about 300,000 years ago.</li>
<li>Modern humans migrated across the world and interbred with Neanderthals.</li>
</ul>
</div>

</div>
</div>

<!-- WORKING -->
<div class="card orange">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-cog"></i>
Working
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Human evolution occurred through gradual genetic changes over millions of years.</li>
<li>Environmental pressures and natural selection favored traits that improved survival and adaptation.</li>
<li>Over time, early primates evolved into hominins with larger brains, upright posture, and advanced tool-making abilities.</li>
<li>These evolutionary changes eventually led to the emergence of modern humans (Homo sapiens).</li>
</ul>
</div>
