POWER TRANSMISSION

(Minerals and Energy Resources)

Academic Information

Class: 10

Book Name: Contemporary India – II

Subject: Geography

Chapter: Minerals and Energy Resources

Learning Objectives
  • Understand the process of power transmission.
  • Learn how electricity reaches consumers.
  • Study the role of transmission lines and substations.
  • Understand the importance of efficient power transmission.
  • Recognize methods to reduce energy losses.
Principle
  • Electricity is transmitted from power plants to consumers.
  • High-voltage transmission lines carry electricity over long distances.
  • Substations regulate and distribute electrical power.
  • High-voltage transmission minimizes energy losses.
  • The system ensures efficient and reliable power delivery.
Working
  • Electricity is generated at power plants.
  • Transformers increase the voltage for transmission.
  • High-voltage lines carry electricity over long distances.
  • Substations reduce the voltage as required.
  • Distribution lines deliver electricity to consumers.
  • Homes, industries, and businesses receive the power supply.
Applications
  • Supplies electricity to homes.
  • Powers industries and businesses.
  • Supports regional and national power distribution.
  • Integrates multiple power sources into the grid.
  • Reduces transmission losses.
  • Ensures reliable electricity supply.
