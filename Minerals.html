MINERALS

(Uses, Sustainability & Daily Life)

Academic Information

Class: 7

Chapter: Rocks

Learning Objectives
  • Understand the importance and uses of minerals in daily life.
  • Learn how minerals are used in construction, healthcare, and industries.
  • Discuss the environmental and ethical issues related to mining.
  • Develop awareness about sustainable use of natural resources.
Learning Outcomes
  • Identify common minerals and their uses in everyday life.
  • Explain the role of minerals in construction, healthcare, and industries.
  • Recognize the environmental impacts of mining and the need for sustainable practices.
  • Appreciate the importance of minerals as valuable natural resources.
Applications

🏗️ 1. Construction

  • Minerals such as limestone, gypsum, and quartz are used in building materials.
  • Essential for making cement, glass, ceramics, and concrete.

🏥 2. Healthcare

  • Minerals are used in medical equipment and pharmaceutical products.
  • Minerals like calcium, magnesium, and zinc are important for human health and body functions.

🏭 3. Industries

  • Minerals are widely used in manufacturing, electronics, transportation, and energy production.
  • They are important raw materials for many everyday products.
