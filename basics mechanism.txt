<h4><i class="fas fa-balance-scale"></i> Lever</h4>
<ul>
<li>Consists of a rigid rod and a fixed fulcrum.</li>
<li>Has three types based on positions of load, effort, and fulcrum.</li>
<li>Works on the principles of torque and conservation of energy.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="mechanism-item">
<h4><i class="fas fa-circle-notch"></i> Pulley</h4>
<ul>
<li>Works on the principle of conservation of energy.</li>
<li>Uses ropes or belts to transmit force.</li>
<li>Redistributes force to lift heavy loads with less effort.</li>
</ul>
