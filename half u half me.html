HALF YOU, HALF ME

(Light, Shadows and Reflection)

Academic Information

Class: 6

Book Name: Science

Chapter No: 2 & 8

Chapter: Sorting Materials into Groups & Light, Shadows and Reflection

Learning Objectives
  • Define transparency and distinguish translucency and opacity.
  • Describe the Law of Reflection.
  • Explain properties of plane mirror image formation.
Science Involved
  • Transparent materials allow light passage, creating clear object visibility.
  • Plane mirrors reflect light following reflection laws, forming virtual images.
Participate & Observe
  • Stand in front of a half-glass, half-mirror exhibit to see a combined image with a friend.
Applications
  • Optics: Demonstrate reflection and transparency.
  • Symmetry: Revealing body symmetry and perspective.
  • Education: Understanding image formation.
