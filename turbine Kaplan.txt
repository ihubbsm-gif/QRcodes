<!-- PRINCIPLE -->
<div class="card blue">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-lightbulb"></i>
Principle of Pelton Wheel Turbine
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Operates on the principle of impulse.</li>
<li>High-velocity water jets strike the turbine buckets.</li>
<li>The water's kinetic energy is converted into mechanical energy.</li>
<li>The buckets deflect the water, causing the wheel to rotate.</li>
<li>The rotational energy is transferred through a shaft.</li>
<li>The shaft drives machinery or electrical generators.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>

<!-- WORKING -->
<div class="card orange">
<div class="card-header">
<i class="fas fa-cog"></i>
Working
</div>
<div class="card-body">
<ul>
<li>Water is directed through a nozzle at high speed.</li>
<li>The water jet strikes the turbine buckets.</li>
<li>The force of the water rotates the wheel.</li>
<li>The rotating shaft transfers mechanical energy.</li>
<li>The connected generator produces electricity.</li>
<li>The used water flows away after striking the buckets.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
